About Us

Making prescription weight loss accessible to everyone.

MyEdessa is a US-based online telehealth clinic specializing in physician-guided GLP-1 weight loss treatment. We connect patients with board-certified providers who prescribe compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide — shipped to your door, without insurance, without hidden fees.

Our Story

Why MyEdessa was founded

MyEdessa was built on a single question: Why does effective, evidence-based weight loss medication cost ten times more than it has to?

Brand-name GLP-1 medications carry list prices above $1,000 per month — largely due to marketing budgets, distributor markups, and cost recovery. The active molecule itself is available to FDA-regulated compounding pharmacies operating under Sections 503A and 503B of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act.

When a provider works directly with those pharmacies, the same medication can be dispensed at a fraction of the brand-name cost — without skipping any clinical oversight that makes treatment safe and effective. That's the model MyEdessa runs.

Our healthcare mission is simple: remove every barrier — cost, access, complexity — that stands between patients and proven weight loss treatment.

Our Process

How your treatment journey works

From first click to ongoing care — here's exactly what to expect.

1

Online Assessment

Complete a 5-minute health questionnaire covering your medical history, current medications, lifestyle, and weight loss goals. Secure and HIPAA-compliant.
