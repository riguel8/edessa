Overview

What is compounded GLP-1?

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a naturally occurring hormone that regulates appetite and blood sugar. When administered as a weekly injection, it helps your body feel full sooner, reduces cravings, and promotes steady, sustained weight loss.

MyEdessa prescribes compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide — the same active molecules found in brand-name medications like Ozempic and Mounjaro — dispensed by US-licensed compounding pharmacies at a fraction of the retail price.

Eligibility

Who qualifies for GLP-1 treatment?

A licensed provider will review your health history, goals, and current medications. If you meet medical criteria, your provider may prescribe GLP-1 as part of your treatment plan.

You may qualify if:

Adults 18+ with a BMI of 27 or higher
Individuals who have tried diet and exercise without lasting results
Those seeking physician-guided medical weight loss

You may not qualify if:

History of medullary thyroid carcinoma or MEN 2 syndrome
Currently pregnant or breastfeeding
History of pancreatitis or severe gastrointestinal conditions
Safety

Important safety information

Warnings & Precautions

Do not use if you or any family members have ever had medullary thyroid carcinoma (MTC) or if you have Multiple Endocrine Neoplasia syndrome type 2 (MEN 2).

Tell your provider if you have a history of pancreatitis, kidney disease, diabetic retinopathy, or gallbladder problems before starting treatment.

Common Side Effects

  • Nausea — most common, typically mild and resolves within first few weeks
  • Constipation or diarrhea
  • Stomach pain or discomfort
  • Headache, fatigue, or dizziness
  • Injection site reactions (redness, swelling)

Your provider will guide you through gradual dose escalation to minimize side effects. Contact your provider immediately if you experience severe or persistent symptoms.

Important: This medication should be used as part of a comprehensive weight management program that includes diet and exercise. This is not a substitute for a healthy lifestyle.
